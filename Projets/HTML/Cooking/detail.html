Pork Kidney with Fermented Chili

  1. Split pork kidney into half from the side, and remove all inner vein completely and slice it thin. Gently give some even cuts on the sliced pork kidney at a 45 degree angle without cutting it off at the way. Then cut across the cuts at a 45 degree angle, this helps to give pork kidney a beautiful “flower blossom “appearance
  2. Marinate sliced pork kidney with salt, black pepper and beer for 10 minutes. Drain and marinate with potato starch, mix well
  3. Slice and chop some garlic, ginger, green onion, Chinese celery, picked chili and fresh chili
  4. Stir fry in a pan filled with adequate amount of heated cooking oil for about 20-30 seconds, and remove quickly from the heat and drain
  5. Put chopped garlic, ginger, picked chili in heated oil to cook in a saucepan for a minute until the aroma comes out, and then add in pre-cooked pork kidney in the sauce to mix together. Simply season it with salt, chicken powder, and garnish with scallion and Chinese celery. Serve warm
